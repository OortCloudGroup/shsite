Governed Reuse

Capabilities can be shared, but calls must be governable and controlled

The platform centrally governs capability calls: who can discover, apply, approve, and invoke capabilities; how much is invoked; and whether usage is compliant are all tracked end to end. Sharing does not mean losing control—it means more efficient reuse under clear rules.

1
Capability PublishingThe capability provider publishes descriptions, APIs, versions, permission scopes, SLAs, and usage examples.
2
Application ApprovalThe caller submits the purpose, project, environment, data scope, and quota request, then approval is routed according to rules.
3
Authorized InvocationAfter approval, credentials, permission policies, and call quotas are issued, and frontend/backend teams integrate according to standards.
4
Monitoring and AuditCall logs, performance metrics, anomaly alerts, and compliance audits are continuously recorded for traceability and optimization.
