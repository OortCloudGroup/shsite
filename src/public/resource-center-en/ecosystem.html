OORT.sh Application Development Technical Standards

OORT.sh helps shape IT governance architecture and provides unified support for business SaaS cloud applications. Using layered, decoupled microservice development as an engineering practice, it breaks down information silos and business stovepipes while achieving application and data decoupling. Layered, decoupled construction has become a must-have requirement — and even an entry threshold — for private-cloud application system development across organizations.
