Distribution Workflow

Close the Android distribution loop from build to receipt

Connect signing, inspection, canary release, push, and installation receipts into a traceable workflow that supports both self-service enterprise store installation and MDM silent deployment.

01

Build and Sign

After uploading APK/AAB, automatically perform version identification, enterprise certificate signing, channel package generation, and integrity verification.

APK/AABv3/v4 Signing

Security and Compliance

Scan high-risk permissions, SDK risks, vulnerable dependencies, and privacy inventories to generate auditable release admission results.

Policy-based Distribution
