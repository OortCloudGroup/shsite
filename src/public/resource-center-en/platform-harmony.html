Overview

HarmonyOS Endpoint: A Next-Generation Intelligent Foundation for Distributed Architecture

HarmonyOS provides OORT.sh with a next-generation intelligent foundation for distributed architecture. Through HAP packaging and AppGallery enterprise distribution, combined with distributed soft bus and Super Device capabilities, it enables seamless cross-device collaboration and application deployment.

Distributed Soft Bus

Seamless device collaboration with low-latency, high-reliability communication.

Atomic Services

Click-to-use without installation, with service cards that flow flexibly.

Super Device

Multi-device integration with drag-to-connect cross-device collaboration.

ArkUI

Declarative UI framework for efficiently building elegant interfaces.
